# Syncd Product Overview

## Product Summary
Syncd is a standalone, music-first dating and social connection app designed to later integrate into the broader Listening Party ecosystem.

It combines:
- profile discovery
- music compatibility
- open messaging
- credits and subscriptions
- video/voice chat
- live games
- Mic Match
- Karaoke Roulette
- trust-based contact unlock

## Core Product Positioning
Syncd should feel:
- premium
- dark and polished
- music-forward
- playful without being childish
- flirty without being cheap

## Core Navigation
Bottom tabs:
1. Discover
2. Chats
3. Games
4. Wallet
5. Profile

## Main Differentiators
1. Music identity and vibe sync matching
2. Streaming-service music import support
3. Mic Match and Karaoke Roulette
4. Trust-based contact unlock
5. Standalone now, absorbable into Listening Party later

## Product Rules
- No fake profiles
- No bots pretending interest
- No deceptive engagement loops
- 18+ only
- Music-first branding
- Open messaging
- Swipe + search/browse
