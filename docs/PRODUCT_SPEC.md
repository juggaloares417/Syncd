# Syncd product spec

## Positioning

Syncd is a music-aware adult dating and social app where compatibility is based on music taste, live interaction, and event-driven chemistry instead of dead profile swiping alone.

## MVP pillars

### 1. Music-first onboarding
- favorite artists
- favorite genres
- top songs
- optional streaming-service import placeholder

### 2. Discovery
- swipe or scroll profile discovery
- compatibility score based on artists and genres
- visible reason for compatibility

### 3. Match-only conversation
- no cold messaging strangers
- private chat unlocks after mutual interest
- event interaction can happen inside live rooms before a match

### 4. Karaoke Roulette
- themed rooms
- timed rounds
- 20 to 30 second intro
- 45 to 90 second karaoke or lyric challenge
- 30 to 60 second mini chat
- quick mutual vibe rating
- rotate to the next partner

### 5. Monetization foundation
- subscriptions
- credits
- premium rooms
- extra entries
- replay or encore requests
- priority placement later

## Core MVP entities
- users
- artist preferences
- matches
- karaoke rooms
- round templates
- credits
- subscriptions
- reports
- blocks

## Room themes to support
- 90s night
- emo night
- country night
- rap / hip-hop night
- love songs
- toxic ex anthems
